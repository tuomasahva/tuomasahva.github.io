PADWORKS

Padworks is MA thesis project by Tuomas Ahva. It explores the possibilities of iPad as musical instrument. Padworks consists of written text and studio live performances of four compositions. All compositions are performed with an iPad. In addition this site contains a list of apps used in the project.
